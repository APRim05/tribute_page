Amancio Ortega

The World's Richest Man (As of September 2016)

A photo of the owner of Zara, the world's richest man - Amancio Ortega.

Ortega's Timeline:

  • Born - 28 March 1936
  • Left school and moved to La Coruña at the age of 14, due to the job of his father, a railway worker.
  • Learned to make clothes by hand whilst working at a local shirt maker called Gala.
  • In 1975, he opened his first Zara store with his wife Rosalía Mera.
  • Ortega keeps a very low profile and, until 1999, no photograph of Ortega had ever been published.
  • In 2011, Ortega retired from Inditex, the parent company of the Zara chain.
  • In 2016, Amancio Ortega was announced the world's richest man by Forbes, surpassing Bill Gates with a net worth of $77.2billion dollars.
  • He has donated millions of dollars to certain charities.
  • Amancio Ortega is now 80 years old.
